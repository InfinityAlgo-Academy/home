Our Story

InfinityAlgo Academy was founded by professional traders who understood the challenges of navigating financial markets without proper tools and education. We experienced firsthand the frustration of using outdated indicators and lacking access to real-time order flow data.

This drove us to create something better - professional-grade tools that provide genuine insights into market microstructure. Starting with our flagship Footprint Price Action Pro indicator, we've helped thousands of traders worldwide gain a competitive edge.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our offerings, always with one goal in mind: empowering traders with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in the markets.
